Four Steps to
Planetary Protection

From raw sensor data to life-saving action — our pipeline processes everything in under 200ms.

1
🔬 Sense
2.3M+ IoT sensors, satellite constellations, and ocean buoys continuously stream environmental data — PM2.5, CO₂, pH, temperature, wind, and 40+ other metrics — into our ingestion layer at 400TB/day.
2
🧠 Analyze
Multi-modal AI models (LSTM, Transformer, Graph Neural Networks) cross-reference sensor streams with meteorological data, historical patterns, and satellite imagery in real-time.
3
🔮 Predict
Probabilistic forecasting engine generates environmental predictions up to 7 days ahead. Extreme event detection runs 24/7 with sub-200ms response latency across the entire sensor grid.
4
🛡️ Act
Automated alert dispatch to government agencies, NGOs, and 1.2B+ citizens via push notifications. Generates localized mitigation playbooks and compliance reports for each affected zone.
